1. Time Management

Create an exam timetable:
Make an exam timetable to properly organize your schedule and increase time management. Include subjects, hours you will study those subjects and ofcourse free time so you wont overdose. Try studying for 25 minutes per subject with a 5 minute break.

eg of a suitable exam timetable:
Monday Tuesday Wednesday Thursday Friday Saturday Sunday
Maths - 25min Science - 25min Geography - 25min Social Studies - 25min Music - 25min Art - 25min Free Day
Break- 5min Break- 5min Break- 5min Break- 5min Break- 5min Break- 5min Math- 25min
History - 25min Business - 25min R.E - 25min I.T - 25min English Language - 25min English Literature - 25min Free Day

2. Note-taking:

There are many ways to take notes but in my opinion the best way is to first listen to the teacher for a few seconds then summarize the information given and write it down. And if a teacher continuously repeats a topic it means that 90% of the time it is coming on the exam so pay attention to those.
